Ramen

Ramen is a classic Japanese food. It contains a delicious soup base that can be made of chicken or pork. There are ramen noodles and some extra toppings such as seed weed and pork.

Sushi

Sushi is also a classic Japanese dish. It usually consists of sushi rice with various ingredients. There can be sashimi on top or veggies wrapped inside.
